Our Franchise

Most burger franchises are expensive to start and complicated to operate. Corbett's Burgers and Soda Bar creates the quality and taste of an expensive burger franchise, with the franchise owner cost and simplicity of a sandwich franchise. Corbett's Burgers and Soda Bar franchises offer:

  • Low Startup Costs

    A Corbett's Burgers and Soda Bar franchise can be opened for as little as $133,400, which includes all equipment, furnishings, location build out, training, three month’s rent, inventory, franchise fee, and working capital.

  • Simple Operation

    Corbett's Burgers and Soda Bar is a simple operation that has three primary positions: Cashier, Bun, and Grill. During a typical peak lunch rush, your store will be staffed by four people.

  • Small Space Requirements

    With our streamlined process, Corbett's Burgers and Soda Bar franchises can operate in as little as 1,200 square feet.

  • Low Cost & Financing for Territory Rights

    We offer financing of the territory fee for our franchisees who want to pursue opening multiple stores. We’re going to help you achieve your goals.

Franchise FAQ's

  • Why own a Corbett's Burgers and Soda Bar franchise?

    We have taken the successful business model from the sandwich and burrito worlds and transformed the burger industry with a simple to operate, low cost franchise model with tremendous growth opportunities. We look forward to sharing more with you about our exciting opportunity.

  • Is there training? Do you provide support?

    Corbett's Burgers and Soda Bar provides an extensive training and support program to help you every step of the way. It all starts with at least three weeks of training at our corporate location for owner operator, and a key employee. This initial training occurs approximately 60 days prior to your restaurant opening and teaches your key staff what they need to help you run your restaurant. When you open your restaurant, we also provide two trainers to assist you at your location for the first seven days. Training continues after your opening with your personal Franchise Business Advisor who works with you step by step on an ongoing basis.

  • How much money can I make?

    The Federal Trade Commission regulates what Corbett's Burgers and Soda Bar is allowed to disclose to potential franchisees. As you proceed through the process of purchasing your Corbett's Burgers and Soda Bar restaurant, you will receive the Franchise Disclosure Document (FDD) that details more about the business opportunity and your expected startup expenses as well as some financial information related to our franchise. We also provide an Earnings Claim that provides you financial information related to our affiliate location.

  • How much will I need to invest?

    The estimated initial investments to establish a Corbett's Burgers and Soda Bar franchise ranges from $133,400 to $277,800. This includes a franchise fee of $23,500 as well as your first three month’s rent, restaurant furnishings and equipment, location build out, small wares, inventory, point of sale system, grand opening marketing, permits, and working capital to open your business. Also available to qualified individuals: a low upfront reservation fee of only $35,250 for 3 units, and $58,750 for five locations.

  • Do you offer financing?

    Corbett's Burgers and Soda Bar does not offer any direct financing, but we can recommend some third-party financing options for you. If you have a retirement fund, we can also recommend some great programs that allow you to borrow against your retirement fund without paying penalties or taxes.

  • What do I need to own a Corbett's Burgers and Soda Bar franchise?

    Working capital of $150,000 with good credit, solid business experience – sales and business experience required, knowledge of the trade area where the store will be located, and a passion for service.
