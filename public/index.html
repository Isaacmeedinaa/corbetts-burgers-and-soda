The Best Burgers in Town!

We want to serve our guests the best burgers and hot dogs in town! That’s why we painstakingly grind our own burgers fresh daily and hand-patty each burger. This is the same fresh grind that goes into our house-made chili for our delicious Nathan’s and Carolina Bright Leaf hotdogs. We also make in-house our creamy buttermilk coleslaw, yummy baked beans, tangy pimento cheese, scrumptious banana pudding, fresh squeezed orangeade, delicious salmon cakes and Corbett’s two secret sauces--Tangy Ranch and BBQ Island.
